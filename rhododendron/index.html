Rhododendron macrophyllum

Pacific Rhododendron

Otherwise known as the California Rhododendron

Size

Shrub or small tree, 3 to 15 feet (1 to 5 m) in height

Range

British Columbia to California; west of the Cascades crest and coastal Washington state

Climate

Fairly dry open forests and edges; commonly grows along roadsides in the rainshadow of the Olympic Mountains
