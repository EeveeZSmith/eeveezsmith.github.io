About Us

Pro Writing Whidbey was founded in 2025 with the aim of making professional writing, editing, and web design services more accessible to small business owners, who may find dealing with large-scale contracting agencies intimidating or cost-prohibitive.

We offer a more approachable option, with personalized services designed to support small businesses, wherever they may be located. Check out our Writing, Editing, and Web Design pages for more information on specific services, or contact us if you have questions!

Meet the Team

Evelyn Smith - Lead Writer, Editor, and Designer

Evelyn has been writing and editing professionally since 2010. She holds a BA in English and a certificate in Professional Science and Technology Writing, both from Washington State University, where she graduated with honors.

She specializes in technical writing and editing, but also has experience in website design and marketing. In her spare time, she writes novels, which she hopes she might one day publish.
