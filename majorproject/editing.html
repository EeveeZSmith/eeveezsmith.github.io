Please contact us for information about editing of projects other than text-based documents, or if you are unsure about which type of editing would best suit your needs.

Service Details:

General Assessment - Reading over a document and giving general feedback on the content, layout, etc. No specific edits provided.

Proofreading - A final "fresh eyes" check on a document that has usually already been edited ato be sure it is ready for publication. Any necessary edits will be submitted.

Copyediting - Most basic form of editing, generally limited to correcting typos, spelling and grammar errors, punctuation, etc.

Substantive Editing - A medium-level edit that includes copyediting plus editing of overall design, layout, and suitability of content for the intended audience.

Line Editing - An extremely thorough edit providing extensive revisions throughout the document.

At the moment, we are not able to offer fact-checking or research services.
